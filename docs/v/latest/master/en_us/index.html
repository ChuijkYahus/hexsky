                              Ship Manipulation

                              Welcome to Navimancy. The art of manipulating ships.
                              Some important keywords before we start.

                              Force is a impulse in a specific direction.

                              Torque is like force. but instead of being in a direction it is spin.

                              Variant a Variant force is one that is based on the ships axis.

                              Invariant a Invariant force is applied based on the worlds axis
